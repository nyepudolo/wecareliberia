About the Program

WE-CARE’s Nurturing Care program works with pregnant women and mothers with children 0 – 3 years doing pre- and post-natal care, including early stimulation and learning up to school readiness. Other activities include: Training of caregivers and teachers, monitoring and coaching, assessment and evaluation and materials development. WE-CARE operates an accredited teacher training program that trains teachers at the early childhood and primary education levels and has conducted C certificate training for more than 1,000 ECE and primary teachers.

Expanding the Vision: Nurturing Care

Building on its successful pilot, WE-CARE has proposed integrating the Family Literacy Program (FLI) with ECD to create a unified initiative called "Nurturing Care." The proposal has been approved for a pilot phase, aiming to enhance the quality and accessibility of early childhood education while fostering a holistic approach to learning and family engagement.

Impact & Future Goals

Through this initiative, WE-CARE aims to cultivate a generation of confident, creative, and critically thinking young learners. By training teachers and engaging families, WE-CARE seeks to build a robust foundation for lifelong learning and community development across Liberia.
